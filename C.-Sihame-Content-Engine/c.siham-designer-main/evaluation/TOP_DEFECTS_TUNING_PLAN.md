# TOP DEFECTS TUNING PLAN

## Rule
Tune only the highest repeated defects first.
Do not expand system complexity before repeated weaknesses are reduced.

## Current likely top defects

### 1. Meaning drift toward generic beauty
**Symptom:** output feels premium but not specific to the post meaning.
**Fix direction:** strengthen post-type gating and symbol relevance.

### 2. Guided-practice under-embodiment
**Symptom:** image looks calm but does not communicate bodily safety or self-contact.
**Fix direction:** require one embodiment cue and one containment cue in every guided-practice direction.

### 3. Educational under-clarity
**Symptom:** educational posts feel elegant but not instructional enough.
**Fix direction:** force stronger title-led hierarchy and explicit concept separation.

### 4. Arabic hierarchy inconsistency
**Symptom:** Arabic text area exists but hierarchy/spacing is not always decisive enough.
**Fix direction:** harden typography placement rules and negative-space requirements.

### 5. Symbol decoration instead of explanation
**Symptom:** symbols are visually pleasing but not semantically necessary.
**Fix direction:** reject symbolic elements that do not directly clarify meaning.
